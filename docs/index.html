How It Works

1

Set your Groq API key

Get a free API key from console.groq.com and paste it in Settings.

2

Hold your hotkey and speak

Press and hold Alt + LShift (or your custom combo) and speak naturally.

3

Transcription appears where you're typing

Release the hotkey. Your speech is transcribed in under a second and typed into your active window.
